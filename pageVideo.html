My YouTube

Here’s a selection of some of my YouTube content.

Comedic Video Edits

These are videos where I take scenes from a show, edit them into jokes, and make a compilation of those jokes. I called them "Dumb Memes" as a form of comedic branding. The most popular of my series of “Dumb Meme” videos is Dumb Oggy and Friends Memes because it got a lot of views in the long-run.




But Every Turn a Different Cover is Used (Gecking Edition)

This is a recurring series of videos based on Friday Night Funkin’, a free and open source rhythm game that's popular and has a lot of mods. In these videos, I mod the game to accompany the characters changing when they sing a new turn. There are other videos like this, so I use the phrase "Gecking Edition" to brand my videos and differentiate them in case if I make a cover of a song someone else covered.




The Great Gecking College Realization

This is a video I made for my 2D Design class. We were supposed to make a video that told a story, based on a genre, and had to use still-images. I ended up making my video my experience in college up to that point because I was majoring in Computer Science, wasn’t interested in the Computer Science classes, and ended up changing my major to Graphic Design (with a focus on animation).
